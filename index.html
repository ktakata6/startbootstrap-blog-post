Plant Identification

by Cruzin on da ʻāina

Posted on June 17, 2019 @2:30pm

What is the Inaturalist app? The Inaturalist app is an app that takes pictures of things of the nature and gives you the meaning or what type of plant it is. It is an amazing app that I can use when I donʻt know the name of a plant.

My experience with this app was easy and fun. I took pictures of plants that I didnʻt know about and it gave me a discription of a plant that looks or comes close to the plant that I took a picture of..

This experience was super fun, and got me very excited to want to take more pictures at work or home.

Download Inaturalist Now, and learn about the plants around your house.
